CampusPulse was made for UNSW students to connect to each other - through spontaneous and immediate events, like study sessions, pickup sport games or meetups. The goal is to make it easy and simple to meet new people, and to do more activities with others when on campus.

Problem

University can be intimidating and isolating. Students may find it hard to find others with similar interests/hobbies, organise spontaneous activities or to meet new people outside their friend group.

Solution

CampusPulse solves this by allowing students to create time-limited events in seconds, allowing anyone to join. It can show what nearby events are going on, and thus encourages impromptu interactions with other students

Features
Create events with duration, location, and descriptions
Browse and join nearby events
Student authentication (email-based sign up)

Tech Stack
Frontend: React
Backend: Node.js 
Database: PostgreSQL
Auth: Supabase
